Crepes

Ingredients
  • 3/4 flour
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbs melted butter
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
Steps
  1. beat eggs in with milk, butter, vanilla
  2. add sugar and wisk to combine
  3. add flour slowly and adjust amout to make the texture thinner than pancake batter
  4. pour 1/4 cup onto a hot (not smoking) cast iron, crepe pan or non stick, and swirl to cover the bottom
  5. flip once the pan has lost its sheen
  6. adjust consistency if needed by adding milk or flour, and adjust head of the pan such that the bottom of the crepe browns slightly by the time the top looses its sheen
  7. serve immediately with honey, syrup, lemon, sugar, nutella and fruit

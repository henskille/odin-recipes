Carbonara

Bucatini carbonara is a personal favorite recipe of mine. In addition to being delicious, it is also easy to make. More specialty ingredients like guancale can be easily substituted for more easily sourced bacon. The key to getting this recipe correct, is making sure the heavy cream-cheese-egg yolk ratio is correct, and using a double boiler to avoid over cooking the egg.

Ingredients
  • 1/4 lb guancale
  • 1/2 cup grated parmagian cheese
  • 1 lb Bucatini
  • 2 tbs heavy cream
  • black pepper to taste
  • 2 whole eggs and 4 additional yolks
Steps
  1. Place a large pot of water on to boil and salt generously
  2. Cube guancale into half inch pieces, and fry on a skillet until golden and crisp
  3. Mix cheese in with all eggs, heavy cream, and a tablespoon of the reduces guancale fat
  4. Remove pasta when aldente, save one cup of pasta water and return pot to stove
  5. Place a large metal mixing bowl over the top of the pot and bring water to boil
  6. Add pasta into the bowl, along with egg mixture and guancale
  7. Stir rapidly, adding pasta water slowly
  8. Once the sauce thickens remove from heat, do not overcook
  9. Serve immediately with leftover cheese and more black pepper
